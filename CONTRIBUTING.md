# Contributing

This project is in an early state. Feedback and small fixes are especially welcome.

- **Issues** — Open an issue for bugs, ideas, or questions.
- **Pull requests** — PRs are welcome. Keep them focused; we may be slow to review.
- **Reach out** — [hello@newmaterial.co](mailto:hello@newmaterial.co) or [NewMaterial on X](https://x.com/newmaterialco).

Thanks for being kind.
